🎶 Musical Career

Kufali began his musical journey in 2014 as a rapper on Audiomack, releasing freestyles and mixes. He was influenced by Nigerian artists such as Wizkid, Phyno, and Iyanya.

His first single, “True Love”, marked his official entry into the music industry. He released his debut studio album Phenomenal Vibez in November 2022, showcasing his versatile Afropop sound and rap skills.

Notable Songs

🌟 Style & Influences

Kufali fuses Afropop rhythms with modern rap and high-energy performances. His music reflects both traditional Nigerian influences and contemporary African music trends.
